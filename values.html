Values I think highly of

Morality

Nowadays, more and more people are forgetting the importance of morality and even children are not getting proper chance to extablish their own concept of morality. This is the top priority social problem and I'm finding my own way to help this society become healthier.

Sympathy

Many societies(especially in Korea) are getting ill because of hatred and misunderstandings among people. Beneath these problem lies the lack of sympathy. If every single people try to put themselves in other's shoes, hatred can no longer exist. I want to make contents and artworks that makes people learn and act the real sympathy.

Happiness

Each person's life gets its meaning when he truely feels happiness inside. But people live as time flows and barely finds the happiness. I think unless we find the meaning of life through fierce introspection and meaningful actions, our life is no better than just a piece of time.
